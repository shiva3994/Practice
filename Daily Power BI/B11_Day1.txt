Types of Analytics:
- Descriptive - What ?
- Diagnostic - How ? (Relationship of the data)
- Predictive - What happens in the future ? 
- Prescriptive - Action Items for the Business Requirement

Types of Variables in Data
1. Quantitative / Numerical
	- Discrete
		- Absolute Values (whole numbers)
			- No. of people
	- Continuous
		- Decimals, Fractions
			- Height, Weight, Temp
	

2. Qualitative / Categorical
	- Binary 
		- Yes / No, True / False, Pass / Fail
	- Nominal
		- No Heirarchy
			- Names, Blood group
	- Ordinal
		- Heirarchical
			- Small, Medium, Large
			- Grades

3. Time Series Data
